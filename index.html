Our Achievements

Our Esports Journey

From grassroots beginnings to international arenas, here’s our story.

2018 – The Beginning

Our PUBG journey began in 2018, playing small local scrims and community tournaments. With passion and dedication, the team started gaining recognition in the regional scene.

2019 – Regional Growth

We began participating in official PUBG Mobile Club Open (PMCO) qualifiers, achieving consistent results and building a loyal fanbase across South Asia.

2020 – Breaking Through

Despite global challenges, 2020 was a breakthrough year. We secured top placements in national tournaments and proved our capability on larger stages.

2021 – International Stage

Our roster competed in major PUBG Mobile Pro League (PMPL) events, facing some of the best teams in the world and gaining valuable experience.

2022 – Rising Reputation

With refined strategies and strong synergy, we reached podium finishes in multiple tournaments and became one of the most recognized teams in our region.

2023 – Consistency & Growth

We continued to grow, balancing competitive results with strong community engagement, and expanded our presence into new esports ecosystems.

2024 – Nearing the Top

With improved training and international exposure, we solidified ourselves as serious contenders in every event we entered.

2025 – The Present

Now, in 2025, we stand as one of the top PUBG Esports teams, competing at the highest level and inspiring the next generation of players worldwide.
